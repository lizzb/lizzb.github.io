What we believe in

Easy software rules

We believe that good development software must be easier than doing it by hand. Otherwise what’s the point? This philosophy is at the heart of our product development.

We're not going anywhere

We know it sucks when software you depend on disappears. We plan to be around for a while, so you can trust we will be there when you need us.

Empowering you

Our big dream is to enable you to create amazing things on the web or mobile devices. We work hard to make software that lets you do new things, rather than holding you back.

HTML5 is our lifeblood

We believe HTML5 will dominate on mobile just as it has on desktop, and that on both platforms the party is just getting started. Don't bet against open web technologies.

You matter

We want all of our customers to have a great experience using our product and be able to get help at any time. We listen to you since we don't have a board of investors telling us what to do.

We love what we do

Being able to run this company is a dream of ours, and we truly love it. We go to work each day so thankful we are able to do this for a living, and we hope that rubs off on our products.
