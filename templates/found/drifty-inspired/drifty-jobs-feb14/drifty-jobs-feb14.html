Our Story

Drifty was started in 2012 by Ben Sperry and Max Lynch when they launched a simple drag-and-drop development tool for jQuery Mobile development, Codiqa, that made it incredibly easy to prototype and build mobile web apps.

Soon after, Drifty released its second product, Jetstrap which made it possible for Twitter Bootstrap developers to build amazing, responsive websites without having to code.

In early 2013, Drifty was accepted into and joined TechStars, the world-wide startup accelerator where we tripled revenue and substantially grew our user base.

In December 2013, we released our third major project, the open source Ionic Framework, with the goal of changing the way developers build mobile apps. Ionic might be new, but it's already gaining some exciting traction and attention from developers around the world.

Today, our products serve over 250,000 people, from more countries than we even knew existed, and have created some incredibly happy customers.

We are an independent, highly talented team that loves making and selling great software, and we do it our way. Come join us!
